These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory. This Service does not use these “cookies” explicitly. You have the right to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, parts or all of the Service cannot function.
